Plan the trade-in before choosing the next deal

A trade-in can simplify a vehicle transaction, but the current vehicle's market value is only one part of the calculation. Loan payoff, positive or negative equity, condition, mileage, title status, and documentation can affect the final structure.

DJIGIT US helps customers organize this information before combining a trade-in with a new lease, finance, or purchase request.

Information commonly needed

  • Year, make, model, trim, mileage, and condition.
  • Current lender and approximate payoff amount.
  • Title and registration status.
  • Accident history, warning lights, or known damage.

Equity matters

If the vehicle is worth more than the payoff, the difference may help with the next transaction. If the payoff is higher than the value, the negative equity does not disappear and must be addressed clearly before moving forward.
