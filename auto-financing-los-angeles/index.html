Auto Financing Support

DJIGIT US helps customers prepare and organize the information needed for a vehicle financing request.

A clearer financing process

Vehicle price, down payment, credit profile, term, taxes, registration and lender requirements all affect the estimated monthly payment. We help customers define a realistic request before moving forward.

Information commonly needed

  • Preferred vehicle and approximate price range.
  • Target payment and down payment.
  • Employment and income information for the application.
  • Identification, residence and insurance-related details when requested.

Approval, rate and final terms are determined by the applicable lender and depend on the complete application and vehicle selected.
