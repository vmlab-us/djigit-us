Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 16, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how DJIGIT US LLC (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website and submit information through our forms, including credit application forms.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information, such as name, phone number, and email address.
  • Application information you provide in forms, such as address, employment, income, driver license information, and related details.
  • Identifiers and technical data, such as timestamps, browser or device information, and similar metadata.

How We Use Information

  • To review your request and communicate with you about your application or inquiry.
  • To verify information you provide and facilitate financing, leasing, or vehicle-related options through participating dealers, lenders, or financial institutions.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and services.
  • To protect our business, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

How We Share Information

We may share information:

  • With dealers, lenders, and financial institutions for the purpose of evaluating or arranging financing or leasing related to your request.
  • With service providers who help us operate our website and systems, including hosting, forms processing, analytics, and communications.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and property.

Credit Reports

If you submit a credit application, we may obtain consumer credit reports and related information as permitted by law and as described in the authorization language presented in the application. Those authorizations are part of your submission.

Data Retention

We may retain information you submit, including credit application information, whether or not your application is approved, as permitted by law and for legitimate business purposes, including recordkeeping and dispute resolution.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

  • You may opt out of text messages by replying STOP, where applicable.
  • You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of certain information, subject to legal and operational limits.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
