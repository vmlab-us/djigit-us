Vehicle lease and finance guidance

Monthly payment, down payment, credit profile, vehicle trim, mileage allowance, term length, taxes, registration, and current availability can all affect the final result. DJIGIT US helps customers organize these details before moving forward with a vehicle lease or finance request.

Lease support

For customers interested in leasing, DJIGIT US can help review the type of vehicle you need, target monthly payment, expected down payment, term, mileage needs, and availability. Lease terms vary by vehicle, lender, credit profile, taxes, fees, and market conditions.

Finance support
