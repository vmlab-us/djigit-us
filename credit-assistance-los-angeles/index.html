Vehicle Credit Assistance

DJIGIT US helps customers prepare a complete and accurate vehicle credit application and understand the next steps.

Preparing for a lease or finance application

A vehicle credit application commonly asks for identity, address, employment, income and requested vehicle information. Complete and accurate information helps reduce unnecessary delays.

What we can help organize

  • Contact and residence details.
  • Employment and income information.
  • Vehicle, payment and down-payment preferences.
  • Follow-up questions or additional document requests.

DJIGIT US does not promise approval or a specific rate. Approval and final terms are determined by the lender after reviewing the complete application.
